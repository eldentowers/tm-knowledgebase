    1. /wager-battle

/wager-battle

Challenge other players to wager battles for Maiden Medals

Wager battles are a great way to earn Maiden Medals and climb the leaderboard!

Usage


cmd - Base Command

arg - Required Argument

arg - Optional Argument

/wager-battle user-to-battle amount summon-location


/wager-battle with all arguments filled in


Running Wager Battles

There are a few steps before your fight to keep everything fair:

  • Both participants must accept the wager amount and location
  • At the end of the fight, both participants must select the same winner


You'll have to confirm the battle settings before your opponent can accept them


Once you confirm the battle settings, your opponent has to accept the wager battle


Summon your opponent into your world as a red sign


The battle begins! Battle your opponent using your own PvP rules and come back to select the winners
